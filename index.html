Critique

The largest issues with the Web Trend Map 4 are that of familiarity and scale. While the map and its deeper relations may be obvious to those familiar with the Tokyo Metro, the majority of viewers likely are not. To make this information more accessible, without taking away from the aesthetics, an interactive version of the image could be made for the web. By hovering over a station with a pointer, a popup could appear with a short blurb about the company and the station. This would also help to alleviate the other main issue of size at the same time. As a result of displaying so many stations at once, the individual companies are very small. By making the image interactive it would be easy to zoom in and out while panning around the image to absorb all of the information it provides.
